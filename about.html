I'm Dr. Rubisco

        My name is Mason Pysell (pie-CELL). I go by Dr. Rubisco  (roo-BISS-coe) online, but my friends call me Doc. I hail from small towns in the Southeastern United States (hey, y'all!) but I always dreamed of making it in the big city.

I started block coding at age 11, and instantly fell in love. Just a few months later, I was writing console applications in C++!  I've been coding ever since, and I enjoy every minute of it. I can't wait to see where my passion takes me next!
