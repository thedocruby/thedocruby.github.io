Privacy Policy


Dr. Rubisco and his website do not collect, store, or share any data from their visitors, public or private, regardless of personal identity. Any information used to run the site on the client's device is requested as needed, and is not stored, sent, shared, or collected in any way. In addition, the client is prompted when personal data is required to display the site as intended, and the client has the option to restrict acces to their data in every case. No client visiting the site is tracked, and each visit to the site is indistinguishable from another. All users remain completely anonymous to the managerial staff of the website and their associates.
