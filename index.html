My Work

For the past few years, I have spent every spare moment working on major game & tech art projects. These are a few highlights.

Resounding is a groundbreaking audio effects pipeline, where I was the first to use real-time raytracing to bring dynamic effects to a sandbox game.

Now the most famous modded graphics pipeline of all time, I was a part of the Iris Shaders project from the beginning until release, and I enjoyed it every minute.

A fun game of catch built for the Oculus Quest in Unity and UE5, Catch '22 gave me an appreciation for every aspect of developing and releasing a game

Perfectly Procedural

My expert use of procedural assets makes my games more flexible, increases performance, and makes asset creation a breeze. See for yourself!

Shaders & Materials

The space scenes shown here are perfect examples of my experience building procedural shaders and materials. With these, I can generate infinite variations to populate the galaxy in just a few minutes!

Models & Geometry

Shown here are a few renders from my Minecraft Abstractions series, a collection of procedural models designed to capture the essence of blocks from Minecraft, without using any assets from the game.

Animations

Procedural animations like my work on the right prevent hours spent rigging and keyframing, and require less computation at runtime. I am still new to rigging and animation, but I thoroughly enjoy it and plan to continue building my skills going forward.

Clogged Pipeline? No Problem!

My experience as a Technical Artist gives me a unique intuition when it comes to debugging and optimizing pipelines, whether they're for rendering, art, assets, or effects. Have a look!

Graphics & Rendering

My experience building the Iris Shaders pipeline from the ground up taught me about all the features of a good graphics pipeline, and how to efficiently
test and debug one.

Art & Assets

My work in modeling, texturing, and graphic design gave me an appreciation for and understanding of the artist's perspective, which means I will do anything to preserve the artist's vision without compromising performance

Digital Effects

My passion for Math and Science, combined with my experience with both audio and visual effects pipelines means I have a unique understanding of digital effects on on a fundamental level, which is infinitely more valuable than experience alone.

More than projects

My portfolio goes beyond the things I made. The real prizes are the useful skills and experience I gained along the way. And next, I'll build on that experience by making even cooler projects for your studio. I can't wait to get started!
